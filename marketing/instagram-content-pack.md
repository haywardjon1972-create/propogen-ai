## 2. Brand voice

| Do | Don’t |
|----|--------|
| Clear, confident, helpful | Hype / “crush it king” energy |
| Speak to busy freelancers | Corporate jargon walls |
| Show before → after | Fake “10k/month” flex |
| Honest free vs Pro | Hide the paywall |
| Short lines, scannable | Long essays in captions |

**Tone:** Professional friend who ships proposals for a living.  
**Emoji:** Light use (✦ ✓ → ⏱) — not spammy.

**Always CTA variants:**
- “Try free → link in bio”  
- “Generate a sample in one click”  
- “Free draft first. Upgrade only if you want AI.”

## 10. What not to do

- Don’t buy followers  
- Don’t auto-spam comments on others’ posts  
- Don’t promise “guaranteed clients”  
- Don’t post only sales — mix **tips + product + proof**  
- Don’t forget free is real — overselling Pro kills trust
